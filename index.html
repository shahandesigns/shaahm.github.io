About

Hello! I'm an aspiring product designer passionate about creating meaningful experiences that solve real problems with a human centered design philosophy.

With a Masters in Product Design and Development from Northwestern's McCormick School of Engineering, I have worked on a range of intriguing projects. From conducting user research with key stakeholder interviews, creating customer journey maps, ideating concepts, and designing innovative solutions, I look for a deep understanding for why we are building on a great idea that is clearly informed by consumer needs.

I enjoy working with multidisciplinary teams as this provides a great opportunity to learn and grow from different perspectives. I have worked with startups and established companies to design products that are both beautiful and functional. My approach combines user research, strategic thinking, and meticulous attention to aesthetic detail.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring nature in different countries, designing conceptual art, or even making cinematic films. I look forward to working with you!
